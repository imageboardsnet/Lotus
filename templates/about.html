About ImageBoards.net

Thank you for visiting my website!

This is a project I developed to help people in discovering imageboards. Initially, this project began as a frontend for the cdd0 imageboards.json project. However, since it was abandoned in 2022, I have been maintaining my own list.

You can easily add your own imageboard or one that is not on the list. To do this, visit https://blossom.imageboards.net/. You can register with one click. You will be given a Login and Password that are randomly generated. At this point, you will be able to freely add an imageboard to the list or claim one. Be aware that manual verification is applied to every new imageboard; it usually takes place every 1-2 days.

Feel free to contact me at me[@]imageboards.net
